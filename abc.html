Membership information

Please note when buying a membership, the membership will become active at 11am on the next working day. To ensure you have immediate eligibility to purchase tickets, we recommend you purchase your membership before the window opens. 

Ballots

Category A Home Games

  • My Bees Members who are under 18 will be able to enter a ballot for each fixture to be in with a chance of winning a pair of tickets for the selected fixture. 50 tickets will be available in pairs. Find out more here.

  • Bees Overseas will be able to apply to the international ballot for all category A games. 100 tickets will be available. Find out more here.

Category A Away Games

  • All My Bees Members will be able to apply to ballot for category A away games. 5% of the tickets allocated to the away section will be avaialable to My Bees Members via a ballot. Find out more here.

Ticket Exchange

Fans can buy tickets that have been listed for sale by Season Ticket Holders, on the ticket exchange.

Each Fan is eligible to buy one ticket per fixture on the ticket exchange if they have not already bought a ticket for that fixture on eticketing.co.uk/brentfordfc. For Category A fixtures, a member must have 40+ TAPs. For Category B fixtures, all members can purchase regardless of the amount of TAPs they have.

Those that are eligible to access the ticket exchange include:

  • All My Bees Members

  • Bees Overseas Members

  • Season Ticket Holders (48 - 72 hours before kick-off)

You can find out more about the ticket exchange in our FAQs here
